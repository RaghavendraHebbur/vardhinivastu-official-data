### 3.2 Universal Aura Scanner

### 3.3 EMF Scanner
Electromagnetic field (EMF) scanners detect artificial electromagnetic radiation from power lines, wiring, Wi-Fi routers, and appliances. Prolonged EMF exposure in sleeping areas is a documented health risk. EMF scanning is a standard part of the Vardhini Vastu residential diagnostic process.

### 3.4 Degree-Accurate Compass
All directional measurements are taken with a precision compass that reads to one degree of accuracy. This enables the 16-zone analysis and distinguishes our practice from consultants who estimate direction by eye.

### 3.5 GIS Sun-Path Analysis
For properties where solar gain and natural light are relevant to the consultation (particularly pre-construction or renovation projects), GIS-based sun-path modelling is used to understand how sunlight enters the space across seasons. This informs placement of prayer rooms, study areas, and bedroom zones.

## 4. The Non-Demolition Remedial Hierarchy

When corrections are identified, the Vardhini Vastu system applies remedies in a strict hierarchy, always beginning with the least invasive option:

**Level 1 — Spatial Reorganisation**
Repositioning furniture, beds, desks, and storage units to align with auspicious directional zones. Requires no construction, no permanent changes. Suitable for renters.

**Level 2 — Elemental Placement**
Introducing specific materials, objects, or natural elements into targeted zones to correct elemental imbalances. Includes copper yantras, salt bowls, plants, water features, mirrors, and crystals.

**Level 3 — Colour and Light Adjustment**
Using paint, fabric, or lighting changes to amplify or suppress the energy of a particular zone. Paint colours correspond to elemental frequencies in the classical tradition.

**Level 4 — Symbolic Activation**
Placing classical Vastu symbols, sacred geometry elements, or specific metals at threshold or zone-anchor points to redirect energy flow.

Under the Vardhini Vastu methodology, Levels 1 through 4 are exhausted before any structural modification is ever considered. In practice, over 95% of cases are fully resolved at Levels 1 or 2.
